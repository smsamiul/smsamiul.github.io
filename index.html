About Me

I am a researcher working at the intersection of hydroinformatics, GIS/remote sensing, flood and drought risk modeling, and climate resilience. My work leverages geospatial data, machine learning, and modeling techniques to support data-driven and equity-focused decision-making in environmental and infrastructure systems.
