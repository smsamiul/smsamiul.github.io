Research Focus

Urban & Coastal Climate Resilience

Integrated geospatial–hydrologic modeling to assess extremes (flood, drought, sea‑level rise) on infrastructure and communities; equity‑aware indicators of exposure and adaptive capacity.

Hydroinformatics & Decision Support

Open data + ML + remote sensing to deliver decision‑ready insights for policy, emergency managers, and the public.
